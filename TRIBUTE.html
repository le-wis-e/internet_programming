ANGELINA JOLIE

  1. Full Name: Angelina Jolie Voight
  2. Date of Birth: June 4, 1975
  3. Place of Birth: Los Angeles, California, USA
  4. Occupation: Actress, Filmmaker, Humanitarian

Early Life

Angelina Jolie was born on June 4, 1975, in Los Angeles, California. Growing up in a family of artists, she developed a passion for storytelling and performance from a young age. As my sister, she always carried a spark of creativity and determination that inspired everyone around her.

Career

Angelina rose to international fame through her acting career, winning an Academy Award for Girl, Interrupted and starring in iconic films such as Lara Croft: Tomb Raider, Mr. & Mrs. Smith, and Maleficent. Beyond acting, she became a respected filmmaker, directing projects that highlight human resilience and global issues.

Humanitarian Work

What makes her truly remarkable as a sister is not just her success on screen, but her compassion off it. Angelina has served as a UNHCR Goodwill Ambassador and later as a Special Envoy, dedicating herself to refugee advocacy and humanitarian causes. Her commitment to helping vulnerable communities reflects the same kindness and empathy she shows within our family.

Family and personal life

As a sister, Angelina is deeply caring and protective. She balances her public life with being a devoted mother to her six children. Her strength, wisdom, and generosity make her a role model not only to the world but also to me personally.

Legacy

Angelina Jolie’s legacy is one of courage, creativity, and compassion. To me, she is more than a global icon—she is my sister, whose love and guidance continue to shape my life
