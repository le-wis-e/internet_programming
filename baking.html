BAKING

:is a method of cooking food that uses prolonged dry heat, normally in an oven, but also in hot ashes, or on hot stones. The most common baked item is bread but many other types of foods are baked. Heat is gradually transferred "from the surface of cakes, cookies, and breads to their center. As heat travels through, it transforms batters and doughs into baked goods and more with a firm dry crust and a softer center". Baking can also refer to the cooking of other foods such as pies, tarts, quiches, casseroles, vegetables, meats, and even pizzas.

    PROCEDURE FOR BAKING

    1. gather ingredients
    2. mix ingredients together
    3. place ingredients in a baking dish

    Before you place the ingredients in the baking dish,preheat the oven to 180 degrees.

    1. Bake in an oven for an hour
    2. Remove from oven
    3. Allow to stand for 10 minutes
    4. Serve and enjoy!
